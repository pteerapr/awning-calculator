Price Calculator Hub

Clean index for standalone awning, screen, pergola, and cassette calculators. Package version: clean-v1 · 2026-05-13

Recommended workflow
Open a calculator, enter size, motor/accessories, quantity, and discount. Use the copy button in the calculator result panel for quotation text.
Maintenance rule
Price tables should be edited separately from calculation logic. Keep a backup before changing motor/accessory formulas.
QA rule
After every price update, test: smallest size, largest size, blank/invalid size, quantity 2, discount, and copy result.
