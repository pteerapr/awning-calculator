Why I did not rewrite every calculator today

The calculators already contain many custom price tables and product-specific rules. Rewriting all logic at once could accidentally break correct formulas. The safest next step is to standardize gradually: first index and packaging, then one calculator at a time.

QA checklist after every edit

  • Input: Width, projection/drop, quantity, discount all trigger auto calculation.
  • Boundary: Test smallest and largest allowed size.
  • Unavailable: Test an unavailable/grey-cell size and confirm warning appears.
  • Motor: Test no motor, Strong Up, and Somfy where applicable.
  • Accessories: Confirm Strong Up accessories do not mix with Somfy accessories.
  • Quantity: Confirm quantity 2 doubles the correct items.
  • Discount: Confirm discount applies after subtotal.
  • Copy: Confirm copy button copies the same information shown in result.
  • Do not: Do not change price tables and calculation logic in the same edit unless necessary.
